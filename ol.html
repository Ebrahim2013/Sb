Steps to download a video site

  1. Open the browser
  2. Go to the video download site
  3. Search for the video url or if it has the option write the name of the video
  4. Click on the download button
  5. Choose the quality
  6. Click on the download button
  7. Chose the destination file
  8. Click on the save button
  9. Wait for the download to complete
